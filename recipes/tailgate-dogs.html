Tailgate ready hot dogs

Ingredients

  • Franks of your choice (Nathan's is the best)
  • chili in a can (Hormels with beans is universally perferred)
  • diced onions
  • mustard
  • buns
  • shredded cheese
  1. put the frankenfurters on a preheated grill about 300 degrees and rotate after 4 minutes
  2. while the dogs are still hot place them on an open face bun
  3. add a liberal amount of mustard
  4. spoon on chili to desired coverage
  5. sprinkle cheese on hot chili
  6. add diced onion
